    Expert Coaching

    Learn from experienced chess masters and FIDE-rated coaches. Whether you’re aiming to master openings, improve endgame tactics, or increase your rating, our tailored classes will help you reach your goals.

    Classes & Courses for All Levels

    From fundamentals to advanced strategies, we offer a variety of flexible courses. Online and in-person sessions make it easy to fit chess into your schedule.

    Events & Tournaments

    Participate in our regular events—from casual club nights to serious tournaments with real prizes. Compete, learn, and make lasting friendships.

    Membership Benefits

    Enjoy exclusive access to training materials, member-only tournaments, private lessons, and discounts in our shop.
